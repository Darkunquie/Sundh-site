What You'll Learn

Eight modules, twelve weeks — every concept reinforced with a hands-on lab on real attacker tooling.

  • OSI model and TCP/IP packet flow
  • Firewalls, IDS, IPS — what they do and where they sit
  • VPNs, VLANs and network segmentation
  • Hands-on packet capture with Wireshark
  • Reading a packet trace like an attacker would
  • Top OWASP risks explained with real CVEs
  • Phishing, social engineering and credential theft
  • Ransomware kill chain, walked end-to-end
  • Supply-chain and insider-threat case studies
  • MITRE ATT&CK framework — the analyst's map
  • Symmetric vs asymmetric cryptography
  • Hashing, digital signatures and PKI
  • TLS handshake, decoded step by step
  • Common crypto failures in production code
  • Key management and HSM fundamentals
  • SOC tiers and the analyst workflow
  • Splunk: searches, dashboards, alerts
  • Writing SPL queries that surface real threats
  • Triage and incident escalation playbooks
  • Threat hunting with Splunk Enterprise Security
  • Reconnaissance with Nmap and OSINT tooling
  • Vulnerability scanning with Nessus
  • Exploitation with Metasploit framework
  • Web app testing with Burp Suite
  • Writing a clear, executive-grade pentest report
  • NIST IR lifecycle: prepare → recover → review
  • Forensic evidence collection and chain-of-custody
  • Containment vs eradication trade-offs
  • Post-incident reports and lessons learned
  • Tabletop exercise: live ransomware simulation
  • Shared responsibility model — who owns what
  • IAM in AWS and Azure, the right way
  • CSPM, CWPP and the cloud security stack
  • Native tools: GuardDuty, Defender for Cloud
  • Securing CI/CD and infrastructure-as-code
  • CEH v12 domain-by-domain coverage
  • CompTIA Security+ exam objectives walkthrough
  • Mock exams with timed simulations
  • Resume polish and LinkedIn rebuild
  • Mock interview rounds with hiring panelists
