Our Story

Why Sunadh Exists

Sunadh Technologies started in 2005 in a small classroom on SAP Street, Ameerpet — a single trainer, a borrowed projector, and a stubborn belief that anyone willing to put in the hours could earn a place in tech, regardless of their college tier or background.

Twenty years on, that belief still drives us. We were built to bridge a specific gap: the one between a non-IT graduate's degree and the practical, project-tested skills hiring managers in Hyderabad's tech corridor actually look for. We don't sell theory. We coach for the interview, the codebase, and the first 90 days on the job.

Today, more than 10,000 students have walked through our doors. Most arrived unsure if a career switch was even possible. They left with a Tableau dashboard, a deployed cloud pipeline, or a working ML model — and a job offer to match.

Our Journey

Twenty Years, Ten Thousand Careers

From a single room in Ameerpet to one of Hyderabad's most trusted IT training names.

  1. 2005

    Founded in Ameerpet

    Started with a single classroom on SAP Street and our first batch of 12 IT trainees.

  2. 2010

    1,000 Students Placed

    Crossed our first major placement milestone, with alumni at Infosys, TCS, and Wipro.

  3. 2015

    Second Branch & Cloud Track

    Expanded to a second campus and added AWS & Azure certification programs.

  4. 2020

    Online + Hybrid Delivery

    Launched live online and hybrid batches during the pandemic — without compromising hands-on labs.

  5. 2026

    10,000+ Trained · 500+ Hiring Partners

    Today, our alumni network spans data, cloud, AI and security teams across India's top tech firms.
