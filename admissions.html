Frequently Asked

Admissions FAQ

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

What documents do I need to enroll? +
A photo ID (Aadhaar / PAN / Passport), one passport-size photograph, and your highest-degree certificate. For working professionals, no employer letter is needed — we accept enrollments directly.
Is there a refund policy? +
Yes — we offer a full refund within the first 7 days of class start, no questions asked. After that, partial refunds are evaluated case-by-case. The free demo class always remains free.
How big are the batches? +
We cap classroom batches at 24 students and online cohorts at 40 to keep trainer attention high. Every student gets a 1-on-1 mentor session at least once per week.
What if I miss a class? +
Every session is recorded and uploaded to your student dashboard within 4 hours. You can also join a parallel batch's session live, or book a 1-on-1 catch-up with the trainer at no extra cost.
Do you provide a certificate? +
Yes — a Sunadh Technologies course-completion certificate, awarded after you submit your capstone project. For tracks like Azure and Cybersecurity, we also coach you through the official vendor exam (DP-203, CEH, etc.) — that certification is issued by the vendor.
